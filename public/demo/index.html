Case No. R6(Wa)XXXX, claim for contract payment. First oral argument session is now open. Plaintiff counsel, please present your complaint.

We request full dismissal. Plaintiff's own Exhibit 5 states "No Contract" explicitly. No deployment to the designated server was performed as required by Article 8. I will organize the issues. First: whether a contract was formed between the parties. Second: whether the deliverable was completed. Third: whether debt was acknowledged. Plaintiff counsel, please explain the "No Contract" notation in your own Exhibit 5.
PLAINTIFF - Sasaki
"No Contract" merely indicates that no written document was prepared - a formal observation. Under civil law, a contract for work is consensual and does not require written form. The sequence of quotation, work instruction, performance, and "send invoice" statement collectively establishes an implied contract.
DEFENDANT - Kurosaki
Exhibit 5 was prepared by plaintiff's own counsel and submitted to this court. It reflects plaintiff's legal assessment, not merely a formal observation. Three separate pieces of evidence from plaintiff's own side consistently confirm contract non-existence.
JUDGE
Both arguments are noted. The court will require detailed briefs. Plaintiff: specify the exact time, manner, and content of the alleged implied agreement. Defendant: provide the factual basis for Mukai's statement and the 35% calculation methodology. Next hearing date will be set. This session is adjourned.
